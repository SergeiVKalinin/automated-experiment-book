# The Art of Automated Experiment

This book collects notes, figures, and executable notebooks on automated experiments, machine learning for materials science, digital twins, and agentic instrumentation.

## How to use this book

The main chapters are written as readable narrative text. Computational sections are provided as Jupyter notebooks that can be opened and edited in Google Colab.

Figures will be placed directly in the book text, and whenever possible, linked back to the code cells or notebooks that generated them.

## Planned chapters

- Instrument digital twins
- Bayesian optimization for finite-budget experiments
- Lookahead policies and utility functions
- Known versus unknown instrument noise
- Agentic microscope workflows
- Human operators, rewards, and autonomous decision-making
